Description of the German credit dataset.

1. Title: German Credit data


2. Number of Instances:  1000

3. Number of Attributes: 20 (7 numerical, 13 categorical)


4.  Attribute description

Attribute 1:  (numerical)
	      Duration in month

Attribute 2:  (qualitative)
	      Credit history
	      excellent : no credits taken/ all credits paid back duly
          good : all credits at this bank paid back duly
	      neutral : existing credits paid back duly till now
          bad : delay in paying off in the past
	      awful : critical account/ other credits existing (not at this bank)

Attribute 3:  (numerical)
	      Credit amount

Attribute 4:  (qualitative)
	      Present employment since
	      unemployed : unemployed
	      newbie :       ... < 1 year
	      junior : 1  <= ... < 4 years  
	      senior : 4  <= ... < 7 years
	      master :       .. >= 7 years

Attribute 5:  (qualitative)
	      Sex

Attribute 6: (numerical)
	      Age in years

Attribute 7: (qualitative)
	      Housing type
	      
Attribute 8: (qualitative)
	      Job
	      unskilled : unemployed/ unskilled
	      skilled : skilled employee / official
	      highly_skilled : management/ self-employed/
		     highly qualified employee/ officer

Attribute 9: (numerical)
	      Number of people being liable to provide maintenance for


Attribute 10: (numerical)
		 Credit risk
		 0: Bad
		 1: Good
